Q. Why are the proxies so small/big?
A. The proxy size has been chosen to easily fit a proxy inside a sleeved card without touching the edges. However, results may vary depending on your printer and sheet size. Try adjusting the zoom scale in your printer settings if you're not satisfied with the results.
